About

Why I Started Evalve

Photo coming soon

I'm Dr. Chris Sanzeri -- an Ed.D. with years in education leadership. Most recently, I served as Director of Special Education at U-46, Illinois' second-largest district, where I oversaw 1,400+ staff members and led large-scale AI implementation across teams.

I've watched talented teachers drown in manual processes while students wait for services. I've seen districts buy into AI hype from vendors who've never set foot in a school building. And I've seen what happens when AI is implemented thoughtfully -- by someone who actually understands the work.

That's why I started Evalve. I help educators and district leaders implement AI that fits their reality -- from the classroom to the central office. No vendor BS. Just practical guidance from someone who's been in your seat.
